## Feminae aquam viris monuerunt.

---

### **Morphosyntactic Analysis:**

1. **Fēminae**  
   - **Lemma:** *fēmina, -ae* (feminine noun)  
   - **Case/Number:** Nominative plural  
   - **Role in Sentence:** Subject ("The women")  

2. **aquam**  
   - **Lemma:** *aqua, -ae* (feminine noun)  
   - **Case/Number:** Accusative singular  
   - **Role in Sentence:** Direct object ("the water")  

3. **virīs**  
   - **Lemma:** *vir, virī* (masculine noun)  
   - **Case/Number:** Dative plural  
   - **Role in Sentence:** Indirect object ("to the men")  

4. **monuērunt**  
   - **Lemma:** *moneō, -ēre* (verb, 2nd conjugation)  
   - **Tense/Aspect:** Perfect active indicative  
   - **Person/Number:** 3rd person plural  
   - **Role in Sentence:** Main verb ("warned")  

---

### **Translation:**  
*"The women warned the men about the water."*  
*(or more literally, "The women gave a warning to the men concerning the water.")*
