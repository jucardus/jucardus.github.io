## Feminae cum viris aquam spectaverunt.

---

### **Morphosyntactic Analysis:**
1. **Feminae**  
   - **Lemma:** *femina, feminae* (f.)  
   - **Case:** Nominative plural  
   - **Role:** Subject ("The women")  

2. **cum**  
   - **Lemma:** *cum* (prep.)  
   - **Case:** Takes the ablative  
   - **Role:** Preposition meaning "with"  

3. **viris**  
   - **Lemma:** *vir, viri* (m.)  
   - **Case:** Ablative plural (governed by *cum*)  
   - **Role:** Complement of accompaniment ("with the men")  

4. **aquam**  
   - **Lemma:** *aqua, aquae* (f.)  
   - **Case:** Accusative singular  
   - **Role:** Direct object ("the water")  

5. **spectaverunt**  
   - **Lemma:** *specto, spectare, spectavi, spectatum* (v.)  
   - **Tense:** Perfect  
   - **Voice:** Active  
   - **Person & Number:** 3rd plural  
   - **Role:** Main verb ("watched/looked at")  

**Translation:** *"The women watched the water with the men."*
