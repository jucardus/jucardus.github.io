Duress is the use of unlawful threats, force, or coercion to compel someone to act against their free will, destroying their ability to consent freely, which can invalidate contracts or serve as a defense in criminal law for acts committed under imminent threat of serious harm or death. It involves extreme pressure, physical or psychological, that leaves the victim with no reasonable alternative but to comply, preventing true voluntary agreement or action. ~ Gemini IA ~
